- **Social justice:** <br/>
  a goal and a process for society to be better, a society in which the members contributes to the greater good and will be able to meet their needs. they will do the best they can do in order to make society go forward.

- **Systemic oppression theory:** <br />
  oppression occurs when a dominant social group uses their differences to unjustly solidify their social standings and to build an uncontestable system which elevates them over the other groups. This results in the exploitation of the subordinate groups.

- **Circle of Oppression in Western Society:** <br />
  oppression happens on three levels. The personal level refers to the negative judgement of each individual towards another individual of a subordinate group. The cultural level consists of the dominant group's culture being the norm everyone should conform. And the structural level is rooted within the society's functioning, such as governments, education, law, media, and the health care system, in which the society favors the dominant group over the subordinate group.

- **Oppression and Privilege in Western Society:** <br />
  In society, people take on either of the two roles: privileged or oppressed. In the Western society, the privileged are those who conform to the cultural norms of the Western Culture.

  a framework that used to explain how oppression is manifested.
